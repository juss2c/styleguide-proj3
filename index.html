Text Styles

Here are standard standard text styling to be used throughout your Halloween web design projects.

H1: Mainpage heading

  • Font-weight: 700 (bold)
  • Font-size: 26px
  • Font-family: Nosifer

H2: Subheading

  • Font-weight: 500
  • Font-size: 18px
  • Font-family: Eater

P: Paragraph text

  • Font-weight: 400 (regular)
  • Font-size: 14px
  • Font-family: Griffy
